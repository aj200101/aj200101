Maaveeran - Synopsis

Maaveeran is a Tamil movie released on 27 May, 2011. The movie is directed by S.S. Rajamouli and featured Ram Charan, Kajal Aggarwal and Sri Hari Raghumundri as lead characters. Other popular actors who were roped in for Maaveeran are Dev Gill. Indu's father is murdered and Harsha is wrongfully accused; as a result, both of them are abducted. But when Harsha finds that he and Indu have a connection from a prior existence, he decides to make things right.

Cast

  • Ram Charan in a dual role as Harsha and Kala Bhairava
  • Kajal Aggarwal in a dual role as Indira "Indu" and Princess Mithravinda Devi
  • Srihari in a dual role as Sher Khan and Solomon
  • Dev Gill in a dual role as Raghuveer and Ranadev Billa
  • Brahmanandam as Kittu, Indu's neighbor (guest appearance)
  • Sunil as Gautam, Harsha's gullible friend
  • Surya as Pratap Varma, Indu's father
  • Sameer Hasan as Maan Singh
  • Subbaraya Sharma as Guru Devulu
  • Rao Ramesh as Ghora
  • Sarath Babu as King Vikram Singh, Mithravinda Devi's father
  • Hema as Sasikala
  • Chatrapathi Sekhar as Raghuveer's sidekick
